Out of the events organised already, this design thinking workshop provides students an opportunity to work on a real-world problem, find a solution and implement it in the given time period.

What is the program?

Technically an internship, the design thinking workshop is about using design thinking methodologies to solve real world problem statements. The problems will be provided by AKEBI as a targeted, well-formed statement.

  • Each team spends 8 weeks coming up with a solution to the given problem
  • Teams must use the methodologies and design thinking approaches outlined in the initial two day workshop
  • Biweekly / Bimonthly meetings will take place to monitor your progress
  • Get an internship certificate at the end of the workshop!
